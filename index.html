Visitor Tips

  • Dress Code: Shoulders and knees covered. No hats inside.
  • Best Time: Early morning or late afternoon to avoid crowds.
  • Tickets: Book Vatican Museums online in advance.
  • Free Entry: St. Peter's Basilica is free (security line).
  • Papal Audience: Wednesdays — free tickets via Prefecture.
